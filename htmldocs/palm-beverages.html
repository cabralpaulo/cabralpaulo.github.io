Palm Beverage Project

What is Palm Beverages?

The Palm Beverages Co products bring a variety of energetic sodas flavor for people who needs to refresh during this summer. We going to start with flavor lemon, cherry, and watermelon. The can of PALM products are designed to be bold, where the consumer can identify it just by the color of the can. It inspired by the Monster energetics cans, which is cool and fun.

The Logo Construction

The name PALM comes from the meaning of the word “leaf of a palm tree awarded as a prize or viewed as a symbol of victory or triumph”. An example of it is when someone is excited, and/or feeling good about something in their life they should drink palm beverages because is the symbol of the victory and the consumer who drink that going feel cool, fresh and winner for the summer 2022.
