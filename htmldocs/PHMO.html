What is Photo & Motion?

Photo & Motion was a team project from Minneapolis College where we need to bevelop a website for the Photographers students that were graduating on May 2023. We needed to share their work and info for each of them. My role in this project was Scrum Master so I was mananging people on this project and helping the team to develop components for the websites, as grid, hover effects in photos, caroussel.
